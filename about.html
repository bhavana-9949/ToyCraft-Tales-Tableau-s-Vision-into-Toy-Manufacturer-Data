About the Toy Industry

The toy industry creates products that educate, entertain, and inspire imagination. From classic puzzles to tech-enabled robots, toys are deeply embedded in childhood growth and happiness. Our goal is to explore toy trends, designs, and preferences using visual data storytelling.

Types of Toys

Electronic & Robotic Toys

High in demand among tech-savvy kids. These toys often include interactive elements like lights, sounds, and AI features.

Education toys

Always a classic choice. These toys are especially popular among preschoolers and elementary kids.

Puzzles & Brain Games

Develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills. Often bought by parents for educational value.

Outdoor & Sports Toys

Popular during summer. Items like bicycles, balls, and outdoor sets boost physical activity and fun.
